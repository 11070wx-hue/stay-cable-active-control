# Code of Conduct

This project welcomes contributions from students, researchers, and engineers
working on structural dynamics and control. Please keep discussions respectful,
specific, and focused on improving the software and documentation.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive when discussing equations, code, tests, and results.
- Credit prior work and avoid overstating validation claims.
- Ask for clarification before assuming intent.
- Respect privacy around unpublished data and lab results.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private data, credentials, or unpublished experimental material
  without permission.
- Fabricating benchmark results, citations, users, or validation claims.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, or close issues and pull requests that violate
this policy. Repeated violations may lead to blocking from the repository.
